Community Guidelines

Effective Date: December 5, 2025

1. Be Respectful and Civil

Our platform is built on a foundation of mutual respect. Treat all members of the MyLibCom community—including authors, reviewers, and fellow readers—with courtesy. Differences in opinion are welcome, but personal attacks, harassment, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated.

  • No Hate Speech: Do not post content that promotes hatred, violence, or discrimination against groups based on origin, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or identity.
  • Constructive Criticism: Keep all reviews and comments focused on the content, not the person who created it or posted the comment.

2. Maintain Content Integrity

We strive for genuine conversations and reliable information. Please ensure your contributions are honest and relevant to the community's purpose (books, reading, and literature).

  • No Spam or Promotion: Do not use reviews, comments, or private messages primarily for advertising goods, services, or external websites.
  • No Misinformation: Do not post false, deceptive, or misleading information, particularly concerning authorship, availability, or pricing.
  • Original Content: Only post content (e.g., in discussion forums) that you have the right to use or create. Respect copyright and intellectual property.

3. Protecting Privacy and Safety

The privacy and safety of our members are paramount. Do not share sensitive information about yourself or others.

  • Personal Information: Do not post private contact information (phone numbers, addresses, personal email) for yourself or anyone else.
  • Reporting: If you see content that violates these guidelines, use the "Report" function immediately rather than engaging with the user.

4. Enforcement

MyLibCom reserves the right to remove any content that violates these guidelines, issue warnings, or permanently suspend accounts. Decisions regarding enforcement are made at the sole discretion of the MyLibCom administration team.

Need to Report a Violation?

Please send a detailed report, including screenshots and links, to our moderation team at admin@mylibcom.co.uk.
